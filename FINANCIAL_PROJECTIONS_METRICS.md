## 📊 Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Paid Channels**
- Google Ads: $3-5 per click, 10-15% conversion = $20-50 CAC
- Facebook Ads: $2-4 per click, 10-15% conversion = $15-40 CAC
- LinkedIn Ads: $5-10 per click, 5-10% conversion = $50-200 CAC
- TikTok Ads: $1-3 per click, 15-20% conversion = $5-20 CAC

**Organic Channels**
- Content marketing: $0 direct cost (time investment)
- Referrals: $0 direct cost (incentivized)
- Community: $0 direct cost (time investment)
- Partnerships: $0-1,000 per deal

**Blended CAC Target**
- Month 1-3: $15-20 (high organic)
- Month 4-6: $10-15 (mix of organic and paid)
- Month 7-12: $8-12 (optimized mix)

---

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Free Tier**
- Conversion to Pro: 15%
- Average LTV: $0 (no direct revenue)
- Value: Referrals, data, engagement

**Pro Tier**
- Average subscription length: 20 months
- Monthly revenue: $9.99
- Affiliate revenue: $0.50/month
- Base LTV: (20 × $10.49) = $209.80
- Plus upsell to Business: 5% × $1,200 = $60
- **Total LTV: $270**

**Business Tier**
- Average subscription length: 36 months
- Monthly revenue: $99.99
- Affiliate revenue: $2.00/month
- Base LTV: (36 × $101.99) = $3,671.64
- Plus add-ons: 20% × $200 = $40
- **Total LTV: $3,710**

**Blended LTV (across all tiers)**
- Free: 80% × $0 = $0
- Pro: 15% × $270 = $40.50
- Business: 5% × $3,710 = $185.50
- **Blended LTV: $226**

---

### LTV:CAC Ratio

**Target:** 3:1 or higher

**Current Model:**
- Blended LTV: $226
- Blended CAC: $12
- **LTV:CAC Ratio: 18.8:1** ✅ Excellent!

**Interpretation:**
- For every $1 spent on acquisition, you get $18.80 in lifetime value
- Very healthy unit economics
- Sustainable business model

---

### Payback Period

**Definition:** Time to recover customer acquisition cost

**Calculation:**
- CAC: $12
- Monthly revenue per customer: $2.50 (blended)
- Payback period: $12 / $2.50 = 4.8 months

**Target:** < 12 months (you have 4.8 months ✅)

## 💡 Break-Even Analysis

### Break-Even Point

**Definition:** When revenue equals total costs

**Calculation:**
- Monthly fixed costs: $1,500 (Month 1)
- Revenue per user: $2.50 (blended)
- Break-even users: $1,500 / $2.50 = 600 users

**Timeline:** Month 1 (achieved on Day 15!)

- High gross margins (89%+)
