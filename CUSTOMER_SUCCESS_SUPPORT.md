## 💬 Support System

### Support Channels

**1. Email Support**
- **Response time:** < 24 hours
- **Hours:** 24/7 (automated responses)
- **Best for:** Non-urgent questions, detailed issues
- **Tool:** Zendesk, Intercom, or custom

**2. Live Chat**
- **Response time:** < 5 minutes
- **Hours:** 9 AM - 6 PM (weekdays)
- **Best for:** Quick questions, urgent issues
- **Tool:** Intercom, Drift, or custom

**3. Help Center**
- **Response time:** Instant
- **Hours:** 24/7
- **Best for:** Common questions, self-service
- **Tool:** Zendesk, Notion, or custom

**4. Community Forum**
- **Response time:** 2-4 hours
- **Hours:** 24/7
- **Best for:** Community support, peer help
- **Tool:** Discord, Circle, or custom

**5. Phone Support** (Pro tier only)
- **Response time:** < 1 hour
- **Hours:** 9 AM - 6 PM (weekdays)
- **Best for:** Complex issues, urgent needs
- **Tool:** Twilio, Zendesk, or custom

---

### Support Ticket Workflow

**Step 1: Ticket Creation**

```
Customer submits support request
↓
Ticket created in system
↓
Automated response sent
↓
Ticket assigned to support agent
```

**Step 2: Triage**

```
Support agent reviews ticket
↓
Categorize issue (bug, feature, question, etc.)
↓
Assign priority (critical, high, medium, low)
↓
Assign to appropriate team member
```

**Step 3: Investigation**

```
Support agent investigates
↓
Gather information from customer
↓
Reproduce issue if applicable
↓
Identify root cause
```

**Step 4: Resolution**

```
Support agent provides solution
↓
Customer implements solution
↓
Verify resolution works
↓
Close ticket
```

**Step 5: Follow-up**

```
Send satisfaction survey
↓
Track customer satisfaction
↓
Identify improvement areas
↓
Update knowledge base if needed
```

---

### Support Response Times

| Priority | Definition | Response Time | Resolution Time |
|----------|-----------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Critical** | Service down, data loss | < 15 min | < 1 hour |
| **High** | Major feature broken | < 1 hour | < 4 hours |
| **Medium** | Minor issue, workaround exists | < 4 hours | < 1 day |
| **Low** | Question, feature request | < 24 hours | < 3 days |

### Churn Prevention

**Identifying At-Risk Customers**

**Warning Signs:**
- No login in 7+ days
- No events created in 30+ days
- No reminders set
- No contacts added
- Negative support interactions
- Feature not being used

**At-Risk Customer Workflow**

```
Customer shows warning signs
↓
Flag as at-risk
↓
Send personalized re-engagement email
↓
Offer help via support
↓
If still inactive, send discount offer
↓
If still inactive, send cancellation survey
↓
If cancelled, send win-back email
```

**Re-engagement Email Template**

```
Subject: We miss you! Here's $5 off

Hi [Name],

We noticed you haven't used [Product Name] in a while.
```
